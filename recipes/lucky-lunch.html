Lucky Lunch

Ingredients

  • 1 Torilla
  • 1 Blue Jazz
  • 1 Sea Cucumber

Steps

  1. Have an upgraded house with a kitchen or cookout kit
  2. Get the 'Lucky Lunch' recipe from The Queen of Sauce on Spring 28, Y2
  3. Ingredient Acquirement
  4. Torilla can be made from 1 corn grown in summer and fall
    • Recipe is learned from The Queen of Sauce on Fall 7, Y1
  5. Blue Jazz is grown in spring
  6. Sea Cucumber is caught in the Ocean from 6am to 7pm in fall and winter
