Seafoam Pudding

Ingredients

  • 1 Flounder
  • 1 Midnight Carp
  • 1Squid Ink

Steps

  1. Have an upgraded house with a kitchen or cookout kit
  2. Get the 'Seafoam Pudding' recipe from reaching Fishing level 9
  3. Ingredient Acquirement
  4. Flounder can be caught at the Beach from 6am to 8pm during spring and summer
  5. Midnight Carp can be caught in the mountain and forest lakes from 10pm to 2am during fall and winter
  6. Squid Ink can be dropped by Squid Kids in the mines. Additionally, it can be produced by Squids and Midnight Squids in a fish pond
