Pumpkin Soup

Ingredients

  • 1 Pumpkin
  • 1 Milk

Steps

  1. Have an upgraded house with a kitchen or cookout kit
  2. Get the 'Pumpkin Soup' recipe from Robin in the mail after reaching 7 hearts with her
  3. Ingredient Acquirement
  4. Pumpkin can be grown in fall
  5. The milk can be from any animal and of any size
